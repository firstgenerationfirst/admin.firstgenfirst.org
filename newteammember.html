Welcome to FGF! As a new team member, you'll be added to the website's Our Team page. To do that, we'll need four things from you:

  1. Your first and last name – pretty self-explanatory
  2. A headshot – doesn't have to be official or professional-looking, just whatever you want your profile picture to be
  3. Your "role" – for example, Jazmin is "Founder," Christian is "Software" (built this website!), and yours can be whatever you think describes your role best; it doesn't have to be official or anything
  4. A short bio – look at the bios of our other team members for inspiration!

Once you have these, please fill out the form below with the information to see what it'll look like on the actual website.

On a larger computer screen, your section will look like this:

–

Contact me at your-email@firstgenfirst.org.

The file you uploaded is too big. Please use an image of 9 MB or less.

On a smaller screen, like a phone or tablet, your section will look like this:

–

Contact me at your-email@firstgenfirst.org.

The file you uploaded is too big. Please use an image of 9 MB or less.
